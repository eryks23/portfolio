How Cooperation Works

My approach to collaboration is simple, transparent, and effective:

  1. Initial Consultation (via messages – SMS/WhatsApp)
    • Purpose: Briefly align on goals and define the project scope.
    • Duration: Usually within 1 business day.
  2. Analysis & Planning
    • Tasks: Define requirements and prepare a roadmap.
    • Duration: 1–2 days, depending on input.
  3. Design (Wireframes & UI Mockups)
    • Tasks: Create wireframes and a clean visual prototype.
    • Duration: ~2 days for small projects.
  4. Development
    • Tasks: Code the front-end and back-end if needed.
    • Duration: 1–2 weeks, depending on complexity.
  5. Testing & QA
    • Tasks: Functional, performance, and basic security testing.
    • Duration: 1–2 days.
  6. Deployment & Support
    • Tasks: Deploy the site/app, assist with launch, and offer post-launch support.
    • Duration: 1–2 days.
